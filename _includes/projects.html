My Projects

Custom SiC traction inverter

For the final year project of my honours degree, I designed a Custom SiC Traction Inverter with my project partner. The 120kW traction inverter is a key component of the electric powertrain on our FSAE vehicle, enabling precise power and torque distribution to the 4 individual motors.

This project is by far the most difficult that I've attempted, it challenged me to acquire extensive knowledge on power electronics, multilayer PCB layout, circuit simulation and so much more.

Co-founding a computer business

In 2020, I started a business selling computers with my friend, we called it Carpio Computers. The business model was simple, we sourced off-the-shelf components from various sources at lower than market rate, built desktop PCs with a strong focus on quality and aesthetics, then sold them at prices much lower than what major retailers were selling for.

Despite accumulating over 30 customers, making tens of thousands of dollars in revenue, the value of the company was much more in providing learning opportunity than in making a profit. We ceased operations about a year and a half later.
